ABOUT IIIT

IIIT Bhubaneswar is a premier technical institution renowned for its cutting-edge research, innovative academic programs, and vibrant campus life. With a focus on nurturing future leaders in technology and management, IIIT Bhubaneswar offers a dynamic learning environment and a diverse community of scholars and students. Located in the heart of Bhubaneswar, the institute is committed to fostering academic excellence and preparing students to excel in the rapidly evolving global landscape.

ABOUT TARS

T.A.R.S. of IIIT-BH, which stands for The Automation and Robotics Society, is a vibrant and dynamic community passionate about the world of automation and robotics. With a vision to excel in the field of robotics and automation, T.A.R.S. is a platform that nurtures innovation, creativity, and technical prowess.
