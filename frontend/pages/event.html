About BotSpark

Get ready for the ultimate robotic showdown! BotSpark is the first-ever State-level Robofest brought to you by TARS-IIIT Bh, in collaboration with the Cultural Society and Mess Society. Thriving over 4 exciting robotics events, BotSpark is your chance to showcase your skills, overcome challenges, and win exciting cash prizes in both technical and non-technical domains.
